## What we collect

- **Account data:** email and name (and similar identifiers you provide).
- **Profile data:** résumé content, preferences, and related information you upload.
- **Conversation data:** messages you send to the HireLoop agent.
- **Billing metadata:** Stripe customer identifiers—we **do not** receive or store full card numbers (Stripe handles card data).

## What we don't collect

We do **not** collect precise location data for advertising, device fingerprints used for tracking, or advertising IDs. We do **not** sell your personal information.

## How we use your data

We use your data to **operate HireLoop** (evaluate jobs, tailor résumés, run scans, provide support) and to **maintain reliability** (logs, error monitoring, security). We do not use your data for unrelated marketing.

## AI training

**We do not train or fine-tune AI models on your data.** We use AI provider APIs (for example **Anthropic** and **Google**) configured with **zero-retention** settings where available, meaning providers do not retain your content to train models.

## Sub-processors

We use vendors to run the product, including (as applicable): **Stripe** (billing), **Anthropic** (Claude), **Google** (Gemini), **AWS** (hosting), and **Inngest** (background jobs). We use agreements appropriate to the services provided.

## Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to **access**, **export**, **correct**, or **delete** your personal data. Contact [privacy@hireloop.xyz](mailto:privacy@hireloop.xyz). We aim to honor GDPR- and CCPA-style requests as a matter of practice regardless of jurisdiction.

## Retention

We retain account data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete personal data within **30 days**, except where we must retain limited records for legal compliance. **Anonymized aggregate job listing data** may be retained to support shared scanning deduplication.

## Security

We use **TLS** for data in transit and industry-standard protections for data at rest. Access to production systems is restricted and monitored.

## Cookies

We use **essential cookies** needed for sessions and security. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies on the marketing site as described in this policy.

## Children

HireLoop is not intended for anyone under **18**.
